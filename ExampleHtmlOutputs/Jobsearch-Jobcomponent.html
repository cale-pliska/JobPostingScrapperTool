JOB TITLE: Junior Front End Developer

POSITION PURPOSE:
Under the direction of the UX Manager, the front end developer will maintain web pages, graphics and online marketing materials for the family of GUESS, INC. websites. This position will be hands-on coding, maintaining templates and creating from scratch. The role will work closely with Web Design, Merchandising and Development teams to ensure deliverables are in-line with high brand standards and best E-Commerce practices.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
Create and maintain front end code for multiple E-Commerce websites (re-designs, seasonal updates, production work, and daily maintenance).
Produce and assist in the deployment of online marketing initiatives, including banner advertisements.
Emphasis will be placed on identifying and implementing innovative, dynamic web solutions, pushing the boundaries of best-in-class E-Commerce websites. This key position will bridge the gap between strong design aesthetics and emerging web technologies.

REQUIREMENTS:
Fundamental understanding of HTML5 and CSS3
Translate a design into a responsive webpage
Practical knowledge of Bootstrap
Adobe Photoshop
Experience with Javascript and plugins
Passionate about writing clean and maintainable code
